# Alien Jump – customisation and legal note

The game is **customised for Cosmic Cafe**: colours, fonts, animated alien background, and the design of the start/game-over screens match the site. In-game graphics (background, platforms, base, spring) are drawn with canvas (no Doodle sprite sheet). Only your alien image (`/alien.png`) is used as an asset.
